FAQ

Everything You Should Know
About Eye Care

How do I book an appointment?
You can book an appointment by calling our clinic directly, or by using the online booking form available on our website's contact page.
Do I need a referral to see an optometrist?
No, you do not generally need a referral to see an optometrist for a standard eye examination.
How often should I have my eyes checked?
We recommend a comprehensive eye exam every 1 to 2 years, depending on your age, risk factors, and physical health.
How long does an eye exam take?
A routine comprehensive eye exam usually takes about 30 to 45 minutes, though it can take longer if additional testing is required.
What should I bring to my appointment?
Please bring your current glasses, contact lenses (and boxes), a list of any medications you are taking, and your insurance information.
